# Numeric Features:
1. GarageCars: Size of garage in car capacity
2. GarageArea: Size of garage in square feet
3. PoolArea: Pool area in square feet
4. MiscVal: $Value of miscellaneous feature (assuming this is a monetary value)
5. MoSold: Month Sold (MM) - assuming this is an integer value
6. YrSold: Year Sold (YYYY) - assuming this is an integer value

# Categorical Features:
1. GarageType:
	* 2Types: More than one type of garage
	* Attchd: Attached to home
	* Basement: Basement Garage
	* BuiltIn: Built-In (Garage part of house - typically has room above garage)
	* CarPort: Car Port
	* Detchd: Detached from home
2. GarageFinish:
	* Fin: Finished
	* RFn: Rough Finished
	* Unf: Unfinished
3. GarageQual:
	* Ex: Excellent
	* Gd: Good
	* TA: Typical/Average
	* Fa: Fair
	* Po: Poor
4. GarageCond:
	* Ex: Excellent
	* Gd: Good
	* TA: Typical/Average
	* Fa: Fair
	* Po: Poor
5. Fence:
	* GdPrv: Good Privacy
	* MnPrv: Minimum Privacy
	* GdWo: Good Wood
	* MnWw: Minimum Wood/Wire
6. MiscFeature:
	* Elev: Elevator
	* Gar2: 2nd Garage (if not described in garage section)
	* Othr: Other
	* Shed: Shed (over 100 SF)
	* TenC: Tennis Court
7. SaleType:
	* WD: Warranty Deed - Conventional
	* CWD: Warranty Deed - Cash
	* VWD: Warranty Deed - VA Loan
	* New: Home just constructed and sold
	* COD: Court Officer Deed/Estate
	* Con: Contract 15% Down payment regular terms
	* ConLw: Contract Low Down payment and low interest
	* ConLI: Contract Low Interest
	* ConLD: Contract Low Down
	* Oth: Other
8. SaleCondition:
	* Normal: Normal Sale
	* Abnorml: Abnormal Sale - trade, foreclosure, short sale
	* AdjLand: Adjoining Land Purchase
	* Alloca: Allocation - two linked properties with separate deeds, typically condo with a garage unit
	* Family: Sale between family members
	* Partial: Home was not completed when last assessed (associated with home improvement)
